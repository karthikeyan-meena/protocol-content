About the app

GermanChunks teaches you the phrases native speakers actually use — at the doctor, in the supermarket, at work, with your landlord, and everywhere else daily life in Germany takes you.

Built on lexical chunking and spaced repetition, the app helps you recall and use real German faster than traditional vocabulary methods.

Features

  • 9 topic packs covering daily life in Germany
  • Chunk cards with example sentences and usage notes
  • Spaced repetition review sessions
  • Scenario challenges for applied practice
  • Streak tracking and weekly progress recap
  • Home screen widget showing your daily due count
  • Fully offline — no account required
